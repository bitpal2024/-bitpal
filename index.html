PROJECT COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT


BITPAL (BPL) is an idea developed by a group of Blockchain and Cryptocurrency ecosystem activists in the Binance Community with the aim of supporting the vision and mission of developing a new network protocol, namely Palapa Chain, where the Palapa Chain Ecosystem development planning aims to unite the vision and mission through collaboration with all entities crypto community in one ecosystem.

In this regard, the role of a strong and mutually supportive community is very important because it is the main pillar and foundation for being able to develop new innovations in creating an expansion of the functions of Blockchain Technology in a wider sector.

Based on this analytical framework, BITPAL (BPL) is here to initiate a community of crypto activists who use the Binance Smartchain Protocol (BNB) to synergize with other Crypto Communities to support the Palapa Chain Network Protocol in developing infrastructure and fundamentals that are able to reach all levels of society.

BITPAL (BPL) then prepared an outline of Program Planning which became the Master Plan for the development of the BITPAL Ecosystem, including the following :

  1. Encourage Blockchain Enthusiast Enthusiasts in the Binance Smart Chain ecosystem to provide full support to Palapa Chain Developers in developing infrastructure that supports cross-community collaboration through the presence of BITPAL (BPL).
  2. Forming competent, creative and innovative human resources through education and training about how to use blockchain technology in various sectors of life to meet society's needs.
  3. Helping Small and Medium Enterprises to be able to adapt to current developments so that they are able to survive and have high competitiveness.
  4. Creating new breakthroughs in Blockchain Technology that can make it easier for people to understand the direction and development of technology so they can compete in the era of globalization.
  5. Develop Blockchain technology projects that involve the active participation of all community members to encourage the creation of strong, independent and technology-literate small and medium businesses.
  6. Connecting all blockchain and Cryptocurrency Enthusiast in one solid ecosystem chain through the development of the Cross-Network Protocol Program.
  7. Encourage non-profit institutions to use Palapa Chain technology as a technological basis for building good and reliable management standards.
  8. Expanding the community activity model to build a stronger and more solid blockchain technology ecosystem..
